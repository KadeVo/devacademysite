Personality

According to the 16personalities test, I am an INFP-A (Mediator). My strengths include empathy, generosity, open-mindedness, creativity, passion, and idealism. I believe these strengths will support me during my journey as being passionate and idealistic will keep me grounded and striving to further improve myself within this forever developing scene.

Culture and Values

The biggest influential figures in my life are my mother and father. As a child, I was very obedient and absorbed whatever they taught me, for better or for worse. I have developed strong values in family, friendship, hard work, integrity, and freedom.

My father's Vietnamese culture had a significant impact on me and shaped my character as they embody the values my parents taught me. An example showcasing my identity is within the workplace. I once had managerial responsibilities and often resolved conflicts. I did my best to bring everyone together to discuss their feelings and get their perspectives known so we could build a mutual understanding of everyone's side.

I knew my coworkers personally, and I understood that if I got them to communicate clearly, the issue would be able to be resolved between them, and it worked wonders. If I had to employ any other strategy, I would consider talking to them one-on-one and getting them to understand that personal matters should be kept out of work and strive to keep it strictly professional in the workplace.

I believe this situation showcases my values as I value their friendship, hard work, integrity, and their freedom to resolve the conflict by themselves without my excessive involvement, enabling the best environment within the workplace.

Limitations

I believe a limitation on myself in terms of my learning and career development will be the potential burnout I may face. The constant grind of the course and my own study may exhaust me. I need to learn to not stress myself and give myself a break, as I may begin to feel like I am not doing enough when the workload becomes more difficult.
