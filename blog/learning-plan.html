My learning plan for the foundation is to dedicate time each day to learn a small chunk of the content. Afterwards, I will self-reflect and review what I have done in the day to ensure it sticks with me.

Each day, I track what I do and how long I have studied using an Excel spreadsheet. I input the time I started, the time I stopped, what content I studied, and how satisfied I am with my progress.

Additionally, I have been reflecting on previous work I have done throughout college and my own self-study so I can further progress my understanding of computer science. I have started watching ComSci lectures just to understand better.

One issue I may face is either burnout or it becoming too difficult that I will lose motivation. This why I am trying to stay ontop of everything so everything won't become so difficult that I will lose motivation to continue. I have identified that I need to take appropriate breaks and be easy on myself as I am switching careers however, I am naturally just hard on myself so it is something I need to work on.

There are a few skills I want to develop while at DevAcademy. I want to really work on my teamwork and communication skills as I know it is a core part of software development. I have studied and graduated with a bachelors of marketing which is primarily group work. However, because of COVID-19 I am a lot worse than I would like to be as the online learning resulted in more solo projects. I can still communicate well and work in teams but I believe it is something I can always be better at.

In regards to workload, I believe I am currently managing fine. I have no current responsibilities and can focus on learning the content within the week. I imagine the workload will increase whwich is why I need to stay more committed to my dedicated hours of studying to reduce the possibility of burnout. I have been managing well due to my Excel spreadsheet that keeps me accountable and keeps me grounded for expectations. Additionally, I have great friends that can help reduce my stress and keep my mind off things that are bothering me!

I would like as much feedback as possible from the Facilitation Team. I am very open-minded and will take in any criticism. I know I am not the best at this nor am I even close to being good which is why I am happy to take in any feedback.
